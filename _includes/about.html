Our Story


Jordan and Nye met while animators on the 2005 feature film "Monster House."

Over the years they remained friends and often spoke about technology, content development, teaching, animation, and AI.

When the virus hit, both of them decided it was time. They created Box Rocket, a studio with the mission to balance great storytelling, new technology research, and a distributed pipeline that facilitates great creative collaboration.
